🥾 Iron Horse Regional Trail

Distance: 32 miles

Difficulty: Easy to Moderate

Description: The Iron Horse Regional Trail follows the route of the former Southern Pacific Railroad line through the San Ramon Valley. Perfect for hiking, biking, and horseback riding.

Trail Highlights

  • Walnut Creek to Pleasanton corridor
  • Connects to BART stations
  • Paved multi-use path
  • Open sunrise to sunset
